What is WUI?


WUI is a project that aims at making lives easy through the use of AI since it is a growing factor in this morden day world. WUI also aims at developing games to help develop and enhance brain IQ and mostly for young kids aged from 2-6 years of age as those are the most cruical years for brain development, also, WUI aims at making games that will benefit the old by means of keeping their minds young, fast, creative and innovative. With WUI you are never too young ,neither are you too old.
